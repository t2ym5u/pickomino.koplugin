## Rules

Roll dice to collect worm tiles. Each turn: roll all dice, set aside at least one face value, re-roll the rest, repeat. Your total (must include at least one worm ⚕) claims the matching tile. Fail to score and you lose your top tile. Most worms wins!

## Rules

- Each turn, roll all 8 dice and keep at least one face value (all dice showing that face are kept).
- The kept face value cannot be reused in the same turn.
- Keep rolling the remaining dice until you stop or are forced to bust.
- You must keep at least one worm-face die to claim a tile.
- Your score equals the highest available tile ≤ your pip total; that tile is removed from the pool.
- You bust (lose your top tile) if you roll no new keepable values, or you stop without a worm die.

## Controls

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| Roll the dice | Tap **Roll** |
| Keep a face value | Tap the face button during the deciding phase |
| Stop and claim a tile | Tap **Stop** |
| New game | Tap **New** |
| Change round count | Tap the rounds button |
| Show rules | Tap **Rules** |

## Scoring

Tiles 21–24 = 1 worm, 25–28 = 2 worms, 29–32 = 3 worms, 33–36 = 4 worms.
